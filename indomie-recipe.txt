Ingredients:
 - 75 cl water
 - 80 g indomie noodles
 - 1 sachet meshed spices
 - 2 pinch of ground pepper

Directions:
  1. In a medium pot, add the water and allow to heat up but not boil.
  2. Add the indomie noodle, cover the pot and allow to boil for 3 mins.
  3. Add spices and ground pepper.
  4. Allow to simmer for a further 2 mins.
  5. Serve!
